Input Format:
	Sixteen Blue Buttons: These buttons will be used as a way for the user to specifiy the coordinate of the tile to make a move on. The first 8 button correspond to the column numbers 1 - 8, and the last 8 buttons will correspond to the row numbers 1 - 8.

Two Black Buttons:
	One for resetting the game (starting from scratch).
	The other is a submit button. The user, after pressing two of the sixteen blue buttons, will press this button to confirm his selection of tile.

8 Segment Display:
	Important informations. Such as who won when the game is over.

2 Red LEDs:
	It shows whose turn it is.

LCD Display:
	The current status of the board.
